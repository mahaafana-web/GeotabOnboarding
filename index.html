Set Up Your Accounts

These are the first things you need to do. Your ERP code and platform access are the foundation of your Geotab partnership.

Your ERP Code

Your ERP code is your internal Geotab account number. You will need it across all partner platforms and for every purchase, billing report, and support interaction. Keep it handy.

Provided in your welcome email

Important: Documents You Received

Along with your welcome email, you should have received your signed Geotab Reseller Agreement, a Finance Welcome Letter, and Payment Instructions. Keep these on file for reference. If anything is missing, contact your Partner Account Manager.

1

Create Your MyAdmin Account

What is MyAdmin? MyAdmin is your back-office platform — it's where you place hardware orders, manage device subscriptions, view billing and invoices, track shipments, submit RMA requests, and access support. Think of it as your operational hub for everything behind the scenes.

Visit myadmin.geotab.com and select "Create an account" to get started.

2

Notify Your Partner Account Manager

Once your MyAdmin account is created, notify your PAM so they can link your ERP code to your account. This step is required in order to access all of your Geotab systems and interact with Geotab.

3

Log In to PX Hub

What is PX Hub? The Partner Experience (PX) Hub is your partner-facing portal — it's where you register opportunities, access the Partner Learning Hub for training and certifications, download marketing and sales resources, and submit support requests. Think of it as your sales and enablement hub.

Single Sign-On: PX Hub now uses single sign-on with MyAdmin — no separate credentials needed. Your MyAdmin email and password are all you need.

To log in for the first time:

  1. Ask your Account Administrator to ensure your MyAdmin profile is Active and Approved with all General Info fields populated.
  2. Verify your correct ERP code is listed first under the Accounts tab in MyAdmin.
  3. Your Administrator assigns you a PX Hub role in MyAdmin (Executive, Manager, or General).
  4. Navigate to the PX Hub login page, select "Geotab Partner" as User Type, and sign in with your MyAdmin email and password.

Important: PX Hub currently supports only one account per user profile. It automatically uses the first account listed in your MyAdmin profile. If your organization’s Executive User is logging in for the first time, they will be prompted to accept the Geotab Partner Program Terms.

Login issues? Email pxhub@geotab.com  |  Full PX Hub Handbook

Go to PX Hub →
4

Add Your Team Members & Assign Roles

Your Account Administrator (the user with the UserManagement.Admin-Users role) manages all user access through MyAdmin under Configurations > User Management. To add a team member:

  1. Have the team member create their own MyAdmin account at myadmin.geotab.com.
  2. Ensure their profile is Active and Approved with all General Info fields populated and the correct ERP listed first under Accounts.
  3. Navigate to Configurations > User Management, find the user, and go to the Roles tab.
  4. Search "PXHub" and assign the appropriate role below.
Role Access Level
PXHub.Access-Executive Highest level — sees all users' data, can view partner scorecards and accept agreements. Only one Executive per organization.
PXHub.Access-Manager Mid level — sees their own data plus all General Users' data. Cannot see Executive or other Managers' data.
PXHub.Access-User Standard level — can only view and manage their own PX Hub data. Most common role for team members.
UserManagement.Admin-Users Account Administrator — can add/edit users and assign all roles in MyAdmin. Required for managing your team's access.

Each user should only be assigned one PX Hub role. If no one in your organization has the Account Administrator role yet, designate one person and ensure they have UserManagement.Admin-Users assigned first.

What is Geotab Academy?

Geotab Academy is a free, self-paced, on-demand learning platform designed to help fleet managers, administrators, and drivers master the Geotab ecosystem. It serves as a central hub for all educational content related to MyGeotab (the fleet management software), Geotab Drive (the mobile app), and Geotab hardware. The Partner Learning Hub inside PX Hub connects directly to Geotab Academy, so you can browse, enroll, and track all your courses from one place.

What You'll Find Inside:

  • Learning Tracks — Curated paths that group related tutorials, videos, and courses together for specific goals (e.g., getting started as a new admin).
  • Step-by-Step Courses — Comprehensive online modules that go deep into features like safety centers, fuel management, and reporting.
  • Interactive Tutorials — Guided simulations where you can practice real workflows in a virtual MyGeotab environment.
  • Quick How-To Videos — Short, focused walkthroughs for specific tasks, such as adding a new vehicle or setting up a zone.

Pricing & Ordering

Understand how Geotab pricing works, set up your own retail pricing, and learn how to place orders through MyAdmin.

Understanding the Pricing Model

How It Works

Geotab uses a bundle pricing model — hardware is included in the monthly subscription fee with no upfront device cost for your customer. Each subscription has a 36-month minimum term with a prorated early termination fee. You purchase at partner pricing and sell at your own retail price.

Available Bundle Plans

Bundle plans range from GPS tracking to full telematics with AI dash cameras. Install bundle variants are also available and include installation services. Review all available plans and pricing in MyAdmin or speak with your PAM.

Promo Codes

Bundle plan pricing requires a promo code applied in MyAdmin when placing orders. Promo codes are available in the pricing templates provided in MyAdmin, or ask your PAM or Geotab team. Without a promo code, standard (non-bundle) pricing applies.

Setting Up Your Pricing

Define Your Retail Pricing

Work with your PAM to review partner pricing for each bundle plan, then set your own retail rates. Define a list price (what you quote customers) and a floor price (the lowest you'll go after discounts). Having these ready before your first sales conversation is critical.

Pricing Calculator

Use the pricing calculator to model your margins. Enter your Geotab cost, set your markup, and calculate what your customer pays and what you earn per vehicle per month.

Installation

Choose how you'll handle device installations for your customers.

Every reseller needs an installation strategy. You have three options — choose one or combine them based on your business model.

Option 1: Become a Certified Installer

Complete the Geotab Installation Certification through the Academy and install devices yourself. This gives you full control over scheduling, quality, and customer experience. Certification is required before performing any installations.

Start Installation Certification →

Option 2: Provide Installation Documents

Provide your customers with Geotab's vehicle-specific installation guides and let them handle installs using their own technicians. Use the Harness Lookup Tool in MyAdmin to identify the correct wiring harness by VIN for each vehicle — it covers light-duty, medium-duty, and heavy-duty makes including Ford, GM, Ram, Toyota, Mack, Kenworth, Peterbilt, Freightliner, Hino, and more.

Harness Lookup (MyAdmin) → Vehicle Install Guides (by Make/Model) →

Option 3: Use the Geotab Installation Network

Leverage Geotab's network of certified installers to handle installations on your behalf. This is ideal if you don't have in-house technicians or need national coverage. Geotab offers Install Bundle Plans — installation packages and pricing are available in MyAdmin.

Harness Lookup & Vehicle Compatibility

Before every installation, use the Harness Lookup Tool in MyAdmin to identify the correct wiring harness for the vehicle. Enter the VIN and MyAdmin will return the exact harness part number. This ensures compatibility and avoids installation issues. For electric vehicles, special installation considerations may apply — consult the vehicle-specific install guide or contact Geotab support.
